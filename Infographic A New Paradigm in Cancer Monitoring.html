The Power of Integration: See the Trend, Optimize Therapy

By tracking CTC dynamics alongside PET/CT results, we shift clinical care from reactive to predictive. The line chart below illustrates a typical patient journey, revealing insights often hidden from imaging alone.

1. Complete Baseline: High metabolic activity on PET/CT is correlated with high CTC counts, confirming an aggressive disease profile that requires immediate action.
2. Confirm Early Response: A sharp decline in CTCs provides a very early signal of treatment efficacy, building confidence weeks before changes are typically visible on a follow-up scan.
3. Detect Resistance Sooner: A rise in CTCs, despite a stable PET scan, serves as a critical early warning for emergent resistance, creating a vital opportunity to adapt the therapeutic strategy proactively.
