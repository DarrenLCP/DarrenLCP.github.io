The Power of Integration: See the Trend, Optimize Therapy

By correlating the real-time cellular dynamics from CTC analysis with the crucial metabolic map from PET/CT, we build a more complete clinical picture. The line chart below demonstrates how CTC trends provide a dynamic narrative that enriches the anatomical insights from imaging, shifting clinical care from reactive to predictive.

1. Complete Baseline: High metabolic activity on PET/CT is correlated with high CTC counts, confirming an aggressive disease profile. Recent work in NSCLC shows that combining Total Lesion Glycolysis with CTC enumeration improves diagnostic AUC to 0.90 versus 0.81 for PET/CT alone.¬π
2. Confirm Early Response: A sharp decline in CTCs provides an early signal of treatment efficacy. In metastatic breast cancer, patients whose CTC count falls below 5 cells/7.5 mL by week 12 enjoy markedly longer overall survival.¬≤
