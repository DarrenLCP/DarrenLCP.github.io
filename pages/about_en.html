About Good Future

Expanding precision medicine capabilities through CTC testing

Good Future was established in 2017. Starting from circulating tumor cell testing technology, the company has gradually built precision medicine laboratory operations, testing workflows, and collaboration capabilities based on stable testing quality and clinical experience.

Its in-house laboratory currently handles blood-sample processing, image analysis, and reporting. At the same time, Good Future continues to integrate automated systems, reagents, consumables, and related workflows to improve service availability and consistency.

Its milestones, certificates, and awards together show Good Future’s recent progress in technology licensing, collaborative development, corporate projects, laboratory accreditation, and overseas expansion.

Milestones

Key Milestones from 2017 to 2025

This update brings together existing product and company information, recent development milestones, and newly added certificate and award records, presenting a complete view of Good Future BioMed’s development history and professional achievements.

2017

Company establishment and technology licensing

  • Good Future was officially established, focusing on the commercialization of CTC negative selection technology.
  • Obtained CTC technology licensing from Chang Gung University, laying the foundation for the company’s core technology.
  • Worked with Inventec Appliances to develop a fully automated CTC detection system integrating cell enrichment, immunofluorescence staining, image acquisition, and AI-assisted interpretation.
  • Starting from academic technology, Good Future built a CTC testing platform designed for standardization, automation, and clinical application.
2019

From medical centers to clinical CTC testing

  • Worked with the Department of Laboratory Medicine at Taipei Veterans General Hospital to introduce the IronMag® fully automated CTC detection system.
  • Built standardized and automated CTC testing workflows based on real clinical testing settings.
  • Continued advancing medical institution collaborations, regulatory permits, and system sales deployment to accelerate commercialization of the CTC testing platform.
2020

From enterprise health screening to national innovation recognition

  • Signed a thousand-person circulating tumor cell (CTC) testing service project with Inventec Group, bringing CTC testing into enterprise health management.
  • Built execution, specimen handling, and service implementation capabilities for CTC testing through large-scale enterprise service experience.
  • The IronMag® automated CTC detection system received the 17th National Innovation Award, demonstrating Good Future’s capability to translate innovative technology into an automated testing platform.
2023

Expanding CTC testing through insurance service scenarios

  • Partnered with Global Life, Nan Shan Life, and other insurance organizations to bring CTC testing into policyholder health management and enterprise customer service scenarios.
  • Used cancer risk assessment and health follow-up as the application core, helping partners build more differentiated health management services.
  • Continued connecting insurance, enterprise, and health-screening channels to expand CTC testing visibility and application depth in the preventive medicine market.
2024

From Taiwan to Southeast Asian CTC testing markets

  • Launched overseas market planning in Malaysia, Thailand, and other markets, promoting CTC testing technology internationally.
  • As part of medical product-line brand integration, the original IronMag® CTC AutoProcessor was renamed Chiline CATCH® CTC Enrichment System, while the core technology and application workflow remained consistent.
  • Advanced regulatory assessment, ISO 13485 quality system preparation, product registration, import/export planning, and local partnership readiness across target markets.
  • Continued connecting overseas distributors, medical institutions, and testing partners to expand international application opportunities for the Chiline CATCH® CTC platform.
2025

Key certifications supporting the international market foundation

  • Received the 2025 National Innovation Advancement Award, continuing recognition from the National Innovation Award and demonstrating Good Future’s ongoing progress in CTC testing technology, automated workflows, and clinical application development.
  • Passed an ISO/IEC 17025 laboratory accreditation scope extension, adding epithelial and mesenchymal circulating tumor cell testing items.
  • The Chiline CATCH® CTC detection system passed the ISO 13485:2016 quality management system, showing that product R&D, manufacturing, and quality control processes meet international medical device standards.
  • Obtained the Malaysia Medical Device Registration Certificate, establishing a regulatory foundation for overseas market introduction and supporting the CTC testing platform’s entry into international application stages.
