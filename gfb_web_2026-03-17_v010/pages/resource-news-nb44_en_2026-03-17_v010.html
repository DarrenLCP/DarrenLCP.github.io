Good Future, a subsidiary of Inventec (2356) Group, has been committed to developing and promoting "circulating tumor cells test" (Circulation Tumor Cell test, hereinafter referred to as CTC test) since its establishment, and has actively expanded cross-industry cooperation. Recently, it has cooperated with Nanshan Life Insurance’s health promotion spillover policy, combining traditional tumor biochemical indicators and advanced CTC detection services to help policyholders understand their own cancer risks in advance.

Cancer has always been the number one killer among the top ten causes of death, but there are no obvious symptoms in the early stage. Therefore, early detection and early treatment are particularly important. CTC detection is one of the powerful tools for early detection of cancer. circulating tumor cells (Circulation Tumor Cell, CTC) refers to cancer cells that enter the blood from the place where the tumor grows and circulate throughout the body with the blood. These cells circulate through the blood to other organs where they have the opportunity to grow into metastatic tumors.

In order to detect CTCs circulating in the blood, Good Future spent five years developing a fully automated circulating tumor cells detection system using exclusive negative screening technology. It can detect extremely rare CTCs in the blood and instantly reflect the risk of cancer. Therefore, CTC detection not only helps in the early detection and early treatment of cancer, but also can be used as an auxiliary detection during the treatment process and tracking. More importantly, only a small amount of blood is drawn during the detection process, so it does not have any side effects on the body.

Good Future General Manager Zeng Qingan said that since Good Future was recognized by the 17th National Innovation Award in 2020, it has not only successfully introduced CTC testing into major domestic medical centers and regional hospitals in the past few years, but has also actively expanded the cancer detection market at home and abroad. Good Future is cooperating with Nanshan Life Insurance’s health promotion spillover policy this time. The insured who has purchased this policy can enjoy free CTC testing services provided by Good Future to assess whether they have the risk of cancer, so as to achieve the purpose of personal health promotion and cancer prevention.

In addition, in order to improve the service experience of policyholders using CTC testing, Good Future has also cooperated with more than a hundred clinics and laboratories, so that policyholders across the province can complete CTC testing nearby.

Chen Weixin, senior vice president of commodity actuarial science at Nanshan Life, said that Nanshan Life's recently launched Nanshan Life Multi-Profit Rate Variable Whole Life Insurance (regular benefit type), in addition to providing life insurance protection, if the insured completes the CTC test within the time limit specified in the terms, he can enjoy the premium payable from the second policy year (excluding riders and additional terms) and endorsement terms) 2% discount to encourage the insured to use CTC detection technology and traditional health check-up tumor biochemical indicators as early as possible to have a more comprehensive understanding of whether they are at risk of cancer, thereby achieving the effect of early detection and treatment, and strengthening the awareness of self-care, which coincides with the health promotion concept that Nanshan Life is committed to promoting.
