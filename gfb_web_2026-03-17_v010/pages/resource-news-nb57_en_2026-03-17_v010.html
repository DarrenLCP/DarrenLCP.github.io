Inventec Appliances and Good Future "2023 Taiwan Medical Technology Exhibition" has achieved fruitful results

"2023 Taiwan Medical Technology Exhibition" was grandly held at the Nangang Exhibition Hall from November 30 to December 3. Inventec Appliances this year continues to focus on "Cloud Health Management-Family Pal Comprehensive Physiological Measurement System", "Smart Medical-TTSmart Infusion System" and "Precision Quasi-medicine - Chiline CATCHcirculating tumor cells automatic detection system" are three major medical and health products. With outstanding product and system maturity, they have won unanimous praise from domestic medical academics and industry professionals, as well as overseas customers from Poland, Thailand, United Arab Emirates, Japan, Singapore, Malaysia, Vietnam and Indonesia.

The exhibition site and visitors in the exhibition hall

"Family Pal Comprehensive Physiological Measurement System" has been launched for nearly four years, and the product line has continued to expand. This year, the latest additions to the product series, "Body Composition Analyzer" and "Smart Belt" were exhibited. In addition to weight, the body composition analyzer can also measure the user's body fat percentage, BMI, body water, protein, bone mass, muscle mass, muscle rate, skeletal muscle mass, skeletal muscle rate, visceral fat, basal metabolism, body age and other data. It will be another tool for users to manage their health. The smart belt was jointly developed with the Taiwan Obesity Health Education Prevention and Control Association. It can detect the user's satiety and eating rate, which will bring diet (force-feeding) management to a new level of precision.

In addition to its domestic promotion, the "Family Pal All-round Physiological Measurement System" has also begun to move overseas. In addition to having obtained medical device certification in Thailand and Vietnam, the certification in Malaysia is also in full swing. We look forward to spreading it overseas in the near future to promote the excellent medical product system developed in Taiwan.

"TTSmart infusion system" is also a highlight of this exhibition. This type of smart infusion system will be an indispensable system for smart hospitals in the future. In the past, it was exclusively owned by large European and American manufacturers. Inventec Appliances is developed and produced by a local team in Taiwan "__TSMART __Infusion Pump" and "TTSmart Infusion Connection System Software" have broken the market monopoly. In addition to continuing to promote and use it with major hospitals in Taiwan, the "TTSmart Infusion System" has also received high praise from Thai customers at this exhibition, and is expected to start overseas expansion in the near future.

In addition to being used in medical institutions, the "TTSmart infusion system" is also suitable for Hospital at Home (HaH) situations. Infusions performed at home can be connected to medical institutions through the cloud, so that remote professional medical personnel can keep track of the home infusion status at any time. In addition to reducing the burden on medical personnel in medical institutions, it can also implement the goal of Hospital at Home (HaH).

Inventec Chairman Ye Guoyi (second from right) and the supervisor of the Health Strategy Council listened to the introduction of TTSmart system

"Chiline CATCHcirculating tumor cells automatic detection system" is also another highlight of this exhibition. In response to the increasing domestic circulating tumor cells testing demand, Inventec Appliances and its subsidiary Good Future exhibited the latest CATCH HT and CATCH Model II, each device can detect up to 96 specimens per day and can perform six biomarkers at the same time. This greatly increases the detection power and will meet the market demand for such accurate and convenient rapid cancer detection.

Good Future Dr. Lin (second from left) introduced "Chiline CATCH" to overseas customers present

This year Inventec Appliances, in addition to the above three major themes, also invites partners to simultaneously exhibit various related products and services. In terms of wound care, Inventec Group AI Center and Taipei Veterans General Hospital jointly developed the "Inventec" smart wound software that has recently obtained Level 2 medical material certification and Taiqiao Medical's "Pharmaceutical Electrospinning", which can be matched with each other as a complete solution for wound care.

Taiqiao medical staff (first from left) demonstrated "medical spinning" in wound care to the public

In addition, Xinsheng's remote health management services and Bio-Bio's "intestinal microbacteria testing services" and "specialized probiotics" have received enthusiastic responses from the public with the most complete product lines. Inventec Appliances relies on decades of experience in the research and development and manufacturing of information communication products, and is committed to the development and application of innovative medical products and services to promote human health and well-being.
