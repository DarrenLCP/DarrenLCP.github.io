"2022 Taitai Smart Medical International Seminar" was held by Changhua Christian Hospital in Bangkok on September 6. This event is the largest smart medical seminar in Thailand after the COVID-19 epidemic. Good Future was invited to give a keynote speech at the scene. Dr. Lin gave a speech on the topic of "Circulating Tumor Cell Detection System (CTC)" to more than 250 Thai medical and health related people present. The wonderful and in-depth introduction received a very enthusiastic response.

Good Future Dr. Lin gave a special speech on "Circulating Tumor Cell Detection System (CTC)"

The theme of this seminar is "Reshaping the New Normal: Focusing on the Application of Smart Technology in Precision Medicine and Regenerative Medicine". Good Future demonstrated the technology of precision medicine with the "Circulating Tumor Cell Detection System (CTC)". Not only did the audience listen with great interest, but they also won the favor of two local Thai hospitals after the meeting. In addition to immediately arranging an online meeting, they even couldn't wait to come to Taiwan to observe CTC equipment. At the booth, we also showcased the Family Pal comprehensive physiological measurement system, which can provide various physiological measurement data such as blood pressure, blood sugar, uric acid, total cholesterol, electrocardiogram, physical and mental energy analyzer (HRV), and blood oxygen/respiration monitoring, and then clearly display it through an integrated APP. The measurement data is stored in the cloud, and historical records can be viewed at any time. The lightweight device has a full range of functions, and has also received cooperation interest from many local Thai manufacturers!

Good Future General Manager Zeng Qingan (middle) and CEO of Changji Overseas Medical Center Gao Xiaoling (second from right)
