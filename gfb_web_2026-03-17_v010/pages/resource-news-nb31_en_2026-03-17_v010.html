Inventec AppliancesDr. Lin was invited to the Chengmei Health Group's [Chengmei Breakfast] live program to introduce the circulating tumor cells test (CTC) to the public in depth. Dr. Lin explained the technical principles in the program and also explained its advantages compared with other conventional cancer detection methods (such as biochemical indicators, imaging, pathological slides, etc.).

For example: circulating tumor cells Testing (CTC) is performed by drawing blood, which is more convenient than traditional pathological biopsy methods, and subjects will not suffer from invasive slices. CTC testing is cheaper than imaging methods (such as CT, PET..., etc.), and it can also avoid exposure to radiation. For people who want regular screening, this type of testing method is very fast and convenient.
