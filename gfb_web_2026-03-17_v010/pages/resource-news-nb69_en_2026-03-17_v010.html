The four-day exhibition ended successfully with a lot of communication, sharing and professional interaction.

This year, we welcomed guests from many Southeast Asian countries to our booth, showing high interest in circulating tumor cells (CTCs) technology, making international precision medicine cooperation more in-depth and forward-looking.

Good Future The topic sharing by a strong team of doctors attracted many professionals and representatives from medical institutions to listen.

The seats were full many times, and many doctors, technicians and researchers from the clinical side took the initiative to stay and communicate, asking in-depth about the clinical interpretation, technical details and application scenarios of CTC.

At the same time, it has also been highly recognized by many domestic medical teams, showing that CTC technology has obvious demand and development space on the clinical side.

International exchange is a highlight of this exhibition.

Thai YouTubers and Thai distribution partners also took the initiative to introduce the applications and advantages of CTC to Thai guests at the exhibition. At the same time, Dr. Lin also shared the value and technical features of CTC in cancer monitoring with Thai doctors, allowing more Southeast Asian medical units to see the depth and quality of Taiwan's technology.

Malaysian distributor WellSpring Solutions also communicated with visitors from Malaysia during the exhibition, sharing the clinical application results and cross-border introduction experience of CTC, allowing more international medical units to understand Taiwan's strength in the field of precision medicine.

Thank you very much to all the friends who came to the booth, communicated with us, asked questions and negotiated cooperation in the past four days.

We will continue to promote CTC and precision medicine-related testing services, and look forward to working with more clinical and international partners to promote medical innovation.

We look forward to seeing you all next time and working together for the advancement of medical technology!
