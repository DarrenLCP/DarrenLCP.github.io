Good Future is a partner of Minsheng Medical Group. After our company's General Manager Zeng Qing'an was invited to attend the 47th anniversary celebration of Minsheng Medical Group on 7/21, today (7/27) Vice President Chen Xinghan of Minsheng General Hospital also visited Good Future, and the two parties had a lively interaction.

Vice President Chen highly praised the Good Future "Chiline CATCH" circulating tumor cells testing service and had on-site blood collection experience. "Chiline CATCH" uses liquid slices [note] for cancer screening. Vice President Chen said that liquid slices are the future trend and can be used as an auxiliary tool for recurrence monitoring and treatment effect evaluation of cancer patients. They can also detect tumors earlier than traditional imaging methods (such as PET, magnetic resonance MRI, and computed tomography CT). It is an accurate and efficient detection method.

Vice President Chen also strongly invited Good Future's "Chiline CATCH" circulating tumor cells fully automatic detection system to be stationed in Minsheng Smart Medical City, so that novel and advanced detection technology and Minsheng Smart Medical City can complement each other, improve the quality of medical treatment and cancer detection, and move forward together in the direction of precision medicine, while also taking care of people's health.

[Note] Liquid section: Using human body fluids (such as blood, urine, spinal fluid, etc.) as detection targets, compared with traditional tissue sections, it has the advantages of non-invasive, representative and instant detection, making it the best tool for auxiliary diagnosis.
