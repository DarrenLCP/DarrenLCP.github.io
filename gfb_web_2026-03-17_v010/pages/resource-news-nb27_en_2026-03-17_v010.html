Good Future's "Chiline CATCH" uses liquid biopsy [note] for cancer screening. It only requires 2c.c. of blood to detect 21 common cancer types in China (such as lung cancer, colorectal cancer, breast cancer, etc.). The Pathology Laboratory Department of Taipei Veterans General Hospital purchased the IronMag®circulating tumor cells fully automatic testing system jointly developed by Inventec Appliances and Good Future in 2019. In the following year, an in-hospital self-funded project was launched, allowing cancer patients to use circulating tumor cells testing to track cancer and monitor treatment prognosis, making "Chiline CATCH" an industry-leading circulating tumor cells testing service.

Today (7/19) Good Future and Min'an Traditional Chinese Medicine signed the "Chiline CATCH" circulating tumor cells testing service cooperation agreement. From now on, people at Min'an Traditional Chinese Medicine can also have circulating tumor cells testing services that are equivalent to those of Taipei Wing General Medical Center.

Min'an Traditional Chinese Medicine's special services include traditional Chinese medicine cancer treatment, traditional Chinese medicine nephrology care, autonomic nervous system disorders, internal medicine (such as chronic diseases such as hyperglycemia, hyperlipidemia, gout and other chronic diseases) and acupuncture. The director, Dr. Li Ailing, has been working on integrated traditional Chinese and Western medicine for many years. For patients, the combination of traditional Chinese and Western medicine can multiply the therapeutic effects. In the field of cancer treatment, Dean Li also has rich clinical experience in treating cancer patients with different types of cancer (such as lung cancer, colorectal cancer, oral cancer, etc.) using the combined treatment of traditional Chinese and Western medicine. Dean Li proactively introduced Good Future "Chiline CATCH" as the cancer screening tool of Min'an Traditional Chinese Medicine. In addition to allowing the public to enjoy high-standard cancer detection services, Min'an Traditional Chinese Medicine also allows Min'an Traditional Chinese Medicine to strictly control the health of the people.

Good Future General Manager Zeng Qing'an said that Min'an Traditional Chinese Medicine has introduced "Chiline CATCH" circulating tumor cells testing service, which also shows that "Chiline CATCH" testing has a wide range of applications. It is not only highly accepted by Western medicine, but also favored by traditional Chinese medicine practitioners. In the foreseeable future, precision medicine combining Chinese and Western medicine will make cancer prevention and anti-cancer more comprehensive, and Good Future "Chiline CATCH" can also work with traditional Chinese medicine to protect people's health.

[Note] Liquid section: Using human body fluids (such as blood, urine, spinal fluid, etc.) as detection targets, compared with traditional tissue sections, it has the advantages of non-invasive, representative and instant detection, making it the best tool for auxiliary diagnosis.
