Chiline CATCH Reagents/Consumables Kits

The Chiline CATCH reagent set uses osmotic pressure differences to remove red blood cells, and then uses specific antibodies to identify white blood cells. It is also equipped with an exclusive patented design of the PowerMag magnetic column to complete the removal of white blood cells. Subsequently, the system automatically performs immunofluorescence staining and image interpretation, and undertakes the complete circulating tumor cells separation process.
