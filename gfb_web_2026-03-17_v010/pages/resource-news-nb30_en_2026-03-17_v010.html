Good Future Dr. Hong Weishan went to the News98 Radio [Famous Doctor Time] live broadcast, and Inventec Appliances Dr. Lin led everyone to learn how to use liquid slides [Note] (circulating tumor cells CTC) for tumor screening. Dr. Lin Shaozhen of Pinxin Clinic was also invited to share her rich clinical experience with the public.

Dr. Lin mentioned that common tumor imaging tests are expensive and require exposure to radiation, or that a low-residue diet or fasting is required before colonoscopy, and processes such as bowel cleansing are also required, which are time-consuming and inconvenient. However, tumor screening using liquid biopsies only requires blood collection, which is relatively convenient and fast.

Dr. Lin also reminded that if you have a family history of cancer, have habits such as smoking and drinking, have a stressful life, or live in areas with poor air quality, it is highly recommended to get a circulating tumor cells test to check your own health.

The three experts gave in-depth and simple introductions in the program, allowing more people to understand circulating tumor cellsCTC’s testing services and providing a new option for public health examinations and cancer screenings.

[Note] Liquid section: Using human body fluids (such as blood, urine, spinal fluid, etc.) as detection targets, compared with traditional tissue sections, it has the advantages of non-invasive, representative and instant detection, making it the best tool for auxiliary diagnosis.
