Extend knowledge

How does the gut-brain axis affect sleep quality?

Mood and gut health are highly correlated. A considerable proportion of serotonin in the human body is produced by the gastrointestinal tract, and serotonin is also an important substance closely related to sleep quality.

When choosing probiotics for sleep maintenance, you can give priority to whether they can produce short-chain fatty acids, tryptophan, GABA and other metabolites related to stress regulation and sleep rhythm.
