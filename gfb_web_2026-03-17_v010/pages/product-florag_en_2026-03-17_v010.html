Warning

Turmeric-Related Interactions and Alerts for High-Risk Groups

  • Those taking anticoagulant and antiplatelet drugs may increase the risk of bleeding.
  • People taking hypoglycemic drugs should be aware of the risk of hypoglycemia.
  • Those who use anti-rejection drugs for organ transplantation need to avoid possible effects on efficacy.
  • Pregnant women, lactating women, those with gallstones, those before and after surgery, those with diabetes, high blood pressure, or those taking multiple medications should consult a physician before supplementing.
