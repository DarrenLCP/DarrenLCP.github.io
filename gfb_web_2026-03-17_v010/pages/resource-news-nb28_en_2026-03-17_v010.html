Taoyuan Minsheng Medical Group held a warm celebration of its 47th anniversary at the "Minsheng Smart Medical City" on July 20. "Inventec Appliances-Family Pal" and "Good Future-Chiline CATCH" were invited to attend the ceremony as cooperative manufacturers.

The "Minsheng Smart Medical City" located near Taoyuan Railway Station was renovated from the Taoyuan Hanpin Hotel. It applies information and communication technology (ICT) to medical care and provides Smart Care that tends to be personalized medical or health management services. The intelligence of this smart medical city will include five major aspects: operating room construction, electronic medical records, telemedicine, image transmission, and AI systems. At the same time, the hotel rooms on the high floors of the original Hanpin Hotel will be retained. It is planned to be equipped with tourist medical care, smart senior residences, and medical staff dormitories in the future. It will become Taiwan's first smart medical city, bringing a new concept of medical services.

In order to achieve the goal of a new smart medical experience, Minsheng Medical also strongly invites Inventec Appliances "Family Pal" to cooperate. Through the remote diagnosis and treatment care technology of Al and the Internet of Things, it can break through the blockade in the post-epidemic era and bring people zero distance from health. In addition, it is also combined with Chiline CATCH"circulating tumor cells fully automatic detection service of Good Future to help medical staff find subtle cancer lesions more efficiently and accurately. Its technology and practice of precision medicine coincide with the philosophy of Minsheng Medical.
