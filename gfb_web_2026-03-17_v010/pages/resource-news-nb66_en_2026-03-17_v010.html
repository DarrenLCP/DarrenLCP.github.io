The 2024 Medical Fair Asia exhibition will be held at the Sands Expo and Convention Center in Singapore from September 11 to September 13. This exhibition is the authorized regional exhibition of the world's largest German MEDICA exhibition in Asia. It can be said to be the largest international medical equipment exhibition in East Asia. In this beautiful garden city, it attracts visitors from Singapore, Malaysia, Thailand, Indonesia, Philippines, Brunei and other Southeast Asian countries, as well as buyers from India, Japan, South Korea and other places.

As always, the Chiline CATCH/Family Pal/TTSmart team of Good Future and Inventec Appliances was a shining star at the venue with its solid product strength. and complete system solutions, attracting the attention of many visitors from all over the world. Through various business matchmaking face-to-face contacts and demonstrations of actual product functions arranged by the exhibition and conference, customers can more effectively understand the characteristics and advantages of the product, which can be said to be the most important first step for future business expansion.

Good Future and Inventec Appliances’s Chiline CATCH/Family Pal/TTSmart team will continue to promote and look forward to carrying forward the Taiwanese medical supplies brand with the highest CP value and letting the world know that Taiwan Can Help.
