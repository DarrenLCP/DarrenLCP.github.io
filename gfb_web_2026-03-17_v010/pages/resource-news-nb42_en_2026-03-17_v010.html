Inventec Appliances and Good Future released three major medical and health products of "Smart Medical", "Cloud Health Management" and "Precision Medicine" at this year's (2022) Taiwan Medical Technology Exhibition, namely "TTSmart Infusion System", "Family Pal Comprehensive Physiological Measurement System" and "Chiline CATCHCirculating Tumor Cell Detection System", each of which is the highlight of this year's exhibition.

Among them, Good Future's "Chiline CATCHcirculating tumor cells detection system" has not only won praises from domestic professionals in the field of precision medicine and cancer detection, but also attracted the attention of doctors from overseas hospitals in Europe, Southeast Asia and the Middle East. Many professionals hope to have further opportunities to discuss cooperation.

In addition, this year’s master forum held during the exhibition invited 12 leading masters in various professional fields to share the applications and benefits of Inventec Appliances and Good Future’s medical technology products in different fields. It also received an enthusiastic response.
