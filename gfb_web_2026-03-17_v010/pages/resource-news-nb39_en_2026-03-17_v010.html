Regarding cancer screening, treatment evaluation, and recurrence tracking, how can circulating tumor cells testing become a new tool for auxiliary diagnosis? As technology advances rapidly, cancer screening tools are constantly updated. The traditional physical biopsy is a shame. In recent years, the new "liquid biopsy" has appeared, which can detect cancer by simply drawing blood.

In this episode of the program, Vice President Zeng Zhiren will use an in-depth and simple way to let the audience understand the application of "liquid biopsy" and "circulating tumor cells" in cancer screening. I hope that after listening to this episode, everyone will have a clearer understanding of "liquid slices" and can also use "circulating tumor cells" for cancer screening in the future!
