Product Highlights

From dietary fiber, prebiotics to enzymes, it fully supports the daily rhythm of the digestive tract

  • Red quinoa provides high dietary fiber, low glycemic and gluten-free properties.
  • Indigestible maltodextrin serves as a water-soluble dietary fiber that assists mineral absorption and the growth of intestinal beneficial bacteria.
  • Isomaltooligosaccharides, inulin and galactooligosaccharides serve as sources of prebiotics and provide food for beneficial bacteria.
  • Comprehensive enzymes cover the decomposition of starch, fat, protein, lactose and fiber.
Ingredient direction

Put high fiber, probiotics and multiple enzymes into the same supplement logic

  • Red Quinoa: High in fiber, gluten-free, and rich in vitamins and minerals.
  • Indigestible maltodextrin: can be used as a source of intestinal microbial utilization.
  • Comprehensive enzymes: Contains protease, lipolytic enzyme, amylase, lactose decomposing enzyme, fiber decomposing enzyme, bromelain enzyme and papaya enzyme.
  • Combined with rice bran concentrate and citric acid, it forms a complete powder package formula.
Recipe Focus

The core ingredient of high fiber and enzyme combination

  • Red quinoa flour: high in dietary fiber, gluten-free, containing trace elements such as calcium and iron.
  • Indigestible maltodextrin: water-soluble dietary fiber, which can slow down the absorption of sugar and fat.
  • Isomalto-oligosaccharides, inulin, galactooligosaccharides: as sources of prebiotics.
  • Comprehensive enzymes: Support the decomposition of starch, fat, protein, lactose and fiber.

FAQ

How is FiberUP suitable for use?

FiberUP takes dietary fiber, prebiotics and enzymes as its core, focusing on digestive tract function and daily fiber supplement; it is not a probiotic product, but it can be used as an alternative to probiotic supplements.
It is recommended to consume it with warm water below 37℃, and please note that the product contains milk and soybean ingredients; if you have allergies, please confirm before supplementing.
