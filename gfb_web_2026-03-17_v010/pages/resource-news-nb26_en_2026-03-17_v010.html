The annual event of Taiwan's family medicine department - the "Taiwan Medical Association of Family Medicine Annual Academic Symposium" was held lively on July 10 at the International Conference Center of National Taiwan University Hospital. Our company also attended this year's grand event and went to the exhibition to introduce Good Future's "Chiline CATCH" circulating tumor cells testing services to all Taiwanese doctors, and communicated and interacted with the doctors on site.

Family medicine physicians, as the health gatekeepers of public family medicine, have given high praise to the "Chiline CATCH" test launched by Good Future, which can detect 21 common cancers (such as lung cancer, breast cancer, and colorectal cancer) in China with only 2 c.c. of blood. For doctors and patients, how to shorten the waiting time for test reports is also very important. Physicians also praise the "Chiline CATCH" test for being able to provide test reports within two days of delivery, and believe that it is a powerful tool that can assist doctors in cancer screening.

In addition, the Inventec Appliances "Family Pal" comprehensive physiological measurement system, which was invited to the exhibition together with Good Future "Chiline CATCH", was also another focus of attention at the exhibition, "__FAMI "LYPAL__" can provide doctors with various physiological measurement data such as blood pressure, blood sugar, uric acid, total cholesterol, electrocardiogram, physical and mental energy analyzer (HRV), and blood oxygen/respiration monitoring. The data is presented through the integrated APP, and the historical measurement data is clear at a glance, making it easier for doctors to manage data and care for patients.
