Good Future「Chiline CATCH」circulating tumor cells Testing service participated in "The Cares Taipei 2022 The 3rd Taipei International Care Technology Application Exhibition"

Good Future "Chiline CATCH" circulating tumor cells testing services and Inventec Appliances "Family Pal" comprehensive physiological measurement system have joined forces to participate in "The Cares Taipei 2022 3rd Taipei International Care Technology Application Exhibition" from 2022/9/15 to 2022/9/17.

Good Future "Chiline CATCH" circulating tumor cells testing services and Inventec Appliances "Family Pal" comprehensive physiological measurement system have joined forces to participate in "The Cares Taipei 2022 3rd Taipei International Care Technology Application Exhibition" from 2022/9/15 to 2022/9/17.

Since cancer has long been the top ten causes of death in China, many health-conscious people at the exhibition were curious about Good Future's "Chiline CATCH" test. They stopped to inquire. After learning that "Chiline CATCH" only requires 2 c.c. of blood to detect 21 common cancers in China (such as lung cancer, breast cancer, colorectal cancer), in addition to praising Good Future's advanced biotechnology, they couldn't wait to leave their information to sign up for the test.

In addition, for care providers, there are many elderly cancer patients who need relatively safe and fast cancer detection services. Therefore, they not only agree with "Chiline CATCH"'s relatively safe detection method using liquid biopsy [note], but also strongly praise Good Future for being able to provide test reports within two days of delivery. Care providers said that providing the report within two days will allow caregivers to grasp information more immediately and help the examinee or cancer patient make proactive and appropriate care arrangements. Therefore, after understanding the details of Good Future "Chiline CATCH", many care providers at the venue not only spoke highly of it, but also immediately started cooperation negotiations with Good Future at the venue!

In addition, the "Family Pal" comprehensive physiological measurement system can provide various physiological measurement data such as blood pressure, blood sugar, uric acid, total cholesterol, electrocardiogram, physical and mental energy analyzer (HRV), and blood oxygen/respiration monitoring. The data is presented through an integrated APP. The data is stored in the cloud and is not limited by distance. It is more in line with the new trend of remote care development and is also a highlight of this Taipei International Care Technology Application Exhibition!

[Note] Liquid section: Using human body fluids (such as blood, urine, spinal fluid, etc.) as detection targets, compared with traditional tissue sections, it has the advantages of non-invasive, representative and instant detection, making it the best tool for auxiliary diagnosis.
