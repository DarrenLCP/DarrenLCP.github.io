Thailand’s PMG Hospital Medical Group (hereinafter referred to as PMG) is a large-scale medical group in Thailand. It has 3 hospitals, 5 clinics, and medical laboratories. It is one of the most iconic medical groups in Thailand.

PMG came to Taiwan to visit Inventec Appliances and Good Future in November this year (2022), and highly affirmed the medical AIoT technology of Inventec Appliances's three major product lines! Inventec AppliancesThe three major product lines are as follows:

1. "CATCHChiline CATCH" circulating tumor cells fully automatic detection service can help local people in Thailand find subtle cancer lesions more efficiently and accurately.

2. The "TTSmart" infusion system is used in chemotherapy wards, isolation wards and various situations that require precision infusion or remote monitoring of infusion. It is expected to bring two major goals to PMG: "improving medication safety" and "improving nurse work efficiency".

3. "Family Pal" is used in various application scenarios such as hospital, remote health care, remote real-time physiological monitoring, home medical care, long-term care, sunshine, etc.

The CEO of PMG Hospital Medical Group said: The technology and equipment of "Family Pal", "TTSmart" and "CATCHChiline CATCH" will become Thailand's PMG in the future The three major tools of Hospital Medical Group can become a great boon to the health of local people, providing the general public with a rare opportunity to protect their health.
