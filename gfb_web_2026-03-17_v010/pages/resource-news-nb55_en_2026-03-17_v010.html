The final stop of the Asian Cup - Bangkok, Thailand

Inventec AppliancesFamily Pal, TTSmart, Chiline CATCH teams and our good partner-Xinsheng Health Management join forces to attack.

Southeast Asia's largest annual medical event was held grandly in Bangkok, the capital of Thailand, from 9/13 to 9/15. Manufacturers from various countries hope to use this prestigious exhibition to showcase their products and services to the Southeast Asian market.

This time "Chiline CATCH" CTC testing and "Flora" probiotics have attracted countless buyers to negotiate, with unlimited business opportunities and full harvest.

Local Chinese medicine practitioners in Thailand said that traditional Chinese medicine combined with high-quality "Flora" probiotics adopted by Taiwanese medical centers (Yadong Hospital, Kaohsiung Wing General Hospital, Zhongshan Affiliated Medical Center) will be the best choice for the Thai Chinese medicine market in the future.

A person suffering from leaky gut suffers from abnormal intestinal digestive function and severe high blood pressure. When he came to the booth, he felt like he had found a treasure, because "Flora" probiotics can enhance his own immunity and the "Family Pal" comprehensive physiological measurement system monitors his physical condition. For people who suffer from both leaky gut syndrome and high blood pressure, it is undoubtedly the best personal butler.

And the "TTSmart infusion system" is not far behind this time. It uses the infusion connection system to improve the work efficiency of nurses during infusion and the safety of medication. The technological strength of Inventec Appliances has amazed manufacturers from all over the world, and they have expressed their willingness to cooperate. Next year will be the first year of "TTSmart" in the international market!

"Family Pal" has added strong support, and Thai distributor Thaiway has sent additional manpower to help. Through Thai explanations, local customers can better understand the value and convenience of Family Pal, creating an unprecedented crowd at the booth.

The final leg of the Asian Cup came to a successful conclusion. Inventec Appliances is definitely the champion among champions and looks forward to continuing to defend the title next year!
