The most representative annual Taiwan Medical Technology Exhibition will be held at Good Future and Inventec Appliances in Nangang from 12/1 to 12/4. This year’s booth size is twice that of last year, displaying a richer and more diverse range of medical products and various innovative applications and services, bringing the latest medical technology to the public (booth number: J318). The exhibition hall opened grandly.

This year, a master forum was held for the first time, inviting 12 leading doctors and experts in various professional fields to take turns for four consecutive days to share the various applications of Inventec Appliances and Good Future medical technology products in different fields, and how to effectively help Taiwanese people move towards health! Don't miss it!
