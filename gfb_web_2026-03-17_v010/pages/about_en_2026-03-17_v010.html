About Good Future

Expanding precision medicine capabilities through CTC testing

Good Future was established in 2017. Starting from circulating tumor cell testing technology, the company has gradually built precision medicine laboratory operations, testing workflows, and collaboration capabilities based on stable testing quality and clinical experience.

Its in-house laboratory currently handles blood-sample processing, image analysis, and reporting. At the same time, Good Future continues to integrate automated systems, reagents, consumables, and related workflows to improve service availability and consistency.

Its milestones, certificates, and awards together show Good Future’s recent progress in technology licensing, collaborative development, corporate projects, laboratory accreditation, and overseas expansion.

Milestones

Key Milestones from 2017 to 2025

Key milestones spanning licensing, system deployment, collaboration, certification, and overseas expansion.

2017

Company establishment and technology licensing

  • Good Future was established, with its preparatory office located in Neihu Science Park.
  • The commercialization plan began with CTC technology licensed from Chang Gung University.
  • Good Future worked with Inventec Appliances to develop an automated circulating tumor cell detection system.
2019

Medical center cooperation and system introduction

  • Worked with the Department of Laboratory Medicine at Taipei Veterans General Hospital to support deployment of the IronMag® automated circulating tumor cell system.
  • The team continued expanding medical-center collaboration, distribution licensing, and system deployment.
2020

Enterprise service project and National Innovation Award

  • Signed a thousand-person circulating tumor cell testing service project with Inventec Group.
  • Received the 17th National Innovation Award in recognition of the IronMag® automated circulating tumor cell detection system.
2023

Insurance-sector partnerships begin

  • Partnered with Global Life, Nan Shan Life, and other organizations.
  • Expand brand visibility in corporate and insurance application scenarios.
2024

Overseas market expansion

  • Began expanding into overseas markets such as Malaysia and continued developing international collaboration opportunities.
  • Advanced both regulatory and market preparation work for testing services and systems.
2025

Laboratory and system certifications and awards

  • Received the 2025 National Innovation Advancement Award.
  • Obtained ISO/IEC 17025 laboratory accreditation and continued strengthening epithelial and mesenchymal testing capabilities.
  • The system obtained ISO 13485:2016 certification and a Malaysian Medical Device Registration Certificate.
